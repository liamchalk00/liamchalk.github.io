Fiberglass Rocket with Advanced Avionics

Flight data measured with thermistors, a GPS, an accelerometer, a magnetometer, and a gyroscope. The rocket was designed to collect altitude, acceleration, velocity, temperature, and rotation during the flight.

Constructed from a Madcow 54mm Adventurer body and used a custom internal avionics bay. The rocket uses a Featherweight Raven 3 as the main flight computer, an Eggtimer Quark as the back-up flight computer, and the Teensy Data Logger to save sensor data on an SD card. Four flights flown with J425, J453, and J530 motors.

Team of four students building hardware, flight electronics, and analyzing flight data.
